The difference between time and attention（时间和注意力的区别）| 翻译

jason fried published on 2023-05-21
A few years ago I realized that if I’m too busy to take something on, I shouldn’t say “I don’t have the time”. In fact, I often do have the time. It’s not that hard to squeeze in some extra time for someone. 几年前我意识到，如果我太忙没法做某些事情，我不应该说“我没有时间”。事实上，我经常有这个时间，为某些人挤出一些额外的时间并不难。 What I don’t have – and what I can’t squeeze in – is more attention. Attention is a far more limited resource than time. So what I should say is “I don’t have the attention”.
